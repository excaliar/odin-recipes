Korean Porkbelly

Korean porkbelly is a similar cut of meat to American bacon. It is a fattier cut which is grilled and then lightly seasoned. Typically it is paired with rice and hot chilli sauces

  • Porkbelly
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Defrost the porkbelly
  2. Grill until fully cooked through
  3. Season according to taste
